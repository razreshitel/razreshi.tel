HexPlor'itel

An offline-first Android map app that turns walking into a personal fog-of-war: draw an area and its grey hexagons clear as you physically move through it. Built for GPS-spoofing-heavy cities, with a filter chain that rejects mock, teleport and low-accuracy fixes before they ever touch your track or coverage.
