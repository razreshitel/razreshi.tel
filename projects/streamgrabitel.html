StreamGrab'itel

An open-source, fully-local in-browser video downloader. It detects media on YouTube, HLS/DASH streams and ~1800 sites, then converts it on your own machine with yt-dlp and ffmpeg — no servers, no uploads, nothing to trust but the source.
