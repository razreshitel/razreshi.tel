Autonomous Landing & Flight Control Agent for KSP

A guidance agent that flies and lands rockets in Kerbal Space Program with zero human input. Live telemetry over kRPC drives a closed-loop controller — suicide-burn timing, throttle and gimbal PID, terminal guidance to a target pad within metres.
